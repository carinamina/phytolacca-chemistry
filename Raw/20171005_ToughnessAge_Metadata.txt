pos		position in greenhouse, used as unique identifier

pop		population of origin; see LatsPopsKey.csv

line		maternal line (nested in population)

indiv		individual replicate (nested in population and line)

mature1		toughness of first replicate mature leaf

mature2		toughness of second replicate mature leaf

young1		toughness of first replicate young leaf

young2		toughness of second replicate young leaf


Definitions:
young leaf is defined as expanding; mature is expanded and toughened up.
toughness is measured as grams of force required to pierce leaf
