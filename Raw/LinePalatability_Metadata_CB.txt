pop		population of origin; see LatsPopsKey.csv

line		maternal line (nested in population)

lat		latitude of origin

age		leaf age (young=expanding or mature=expanded and toughened up)

biomass		average total biomass of caterpillars in a line-level replicate cup at the end of palatability experiment; a caterpillar that died before the experiment ended is included with biomass of 0

surv		average total biomass of surviving caterpillars in a line-level replicate cup at the end of palatability experiment

area		average leaf area consumed in a line-level replicate cup at the end of palatability experiment
